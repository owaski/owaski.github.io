Hi, I'm currently a first year PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. [Lei Li](https://lileicc.github.io/index.html). Previously, I received my B.Eng. from IIIS at Tsinghua Univesity (a.k.a. Yao Class), advised by Prof. [Yi Wu](https://jxwuyi.weebly.com). 

My current research focus is on speech translation, both offline and simultaneous. The ambition is to build a system that can translate speech in real-time, enabling seamless communication around the world. Other than that, I'm also interested in Large Langauge Model (LLM) application and automatic drug discovery.
